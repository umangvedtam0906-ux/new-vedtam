FAQ

Common Questions About ISO Consulting

Get answers on standard processes, certification timelines, and requirements.

How long does ISO certification take?
+
Depending on your readiness and ISO type, it typically ranges from 8 to 16 weeks. We work to accelerate this by minimizing organizational bottlenecks through streamlined documentation and proactive scheduling.
Do you only support ISO 27001?
+
No, we also support ISO 20000 (IT Service Management), ISO 31000 (Risk Management), ISO 22301 (Business Continuity) and can assist in aligning with ISO 9001 (Quality Management).
What size businesses do you serve for ISO consultancy?
+
We work with startups, SMBs, and large enterprises across various industries looking to implement scalable security and quality frameworks that grow with their business.
Will you help during the actual external audits?
+
Yes. We provide full audit readiness support, internal audit services, and hands-on guidance during the external certification audit to ensure a successful outcome.
How do you conduct a gap assessment?
+
We review your current processes against the standard requirements, interview key personnel, and inspect evidence to identify exactly what's missing for successful certification.
What are the primary benefits of ISO 27001?
+
It significantly improves your security posture, builds customer trust, ensures legal compliance with data protection laws, and provides a clear competitive advantage.
Do you provide internal audit training?
+
Yes, we train your internal team to conduct effective audits, ensuring your organization can maintain compliance and continuous improvement long after our initial engagement.
Can you help with multiple ISO standards at once?
+
Absolutely. We specialize in Integrated Management Systems (IMS), allowing you to achieve multiple certifications (like ISO 9001 and 27001) simultaneously and more efficiently.
