Frequently Asked Questions

Get All the Important Answers

Everything you need to know about working with Vedtam Tech Solutions.

What industries do you specialize in?
+
At Vedtam, we work with a wide spectrum of industries including Banking, Financial Services & Insurance (BFSI), Healthcare, Manufacturing, Education Technology, Software-as-a-Service (SaaS), and Small to Medium Businesses (SMBs). Our domain expertise allows us to offer tailored solutions that align with the operational and compliance needs specific to each sector.
Can you help us with ISO certification?
+
Absolutely. We have a proven track record in helping businesses achieve certifications such as ISO 27001, ISO 22301, ISO 20000, and PCI DSS. Our experts guide you through every step - from gap assessments to policy creation, implementation, internal audits, and certification support.
Do you work with startups and small businesses?
+
Yes, we cater to startups and small businesses with cost-effective IT solutions that can scale as you grow. Whether you need basic cybersecurity, cloud setup, or compliance guidance, we create packages that are flexible, efficient, and aligned with your budget and long-term vision.
How do you ensure project success?
+
We follow a structured methodology: Discovery, Strategy, Implementation, and Continuous Support. This ensures every project is aligned with your business goals, delivered on time, and provides measurable value.
Do you provide customized solutions?
+
Absolutely. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all. Every solution we build is customized to your specific technology stack, budget, and operational requirements to ensure maximum impact.
What is your typical project delivery timeline?
+
Timelines vary by project scope. A focused audit or assessment might take 1-2 weeks, while complex software builds or enterprise cloud migrations can take 3-6 months. We always provide clear milestones during the strategy phase.
How do you handle post-implementation support?
+
We offer 24/7 remote support and proactive maintenance plans. Our team continuously monitors your systems for performance and security, providing regular updates and troubleshooting to ensure long-term stability.
Is your team certified in specific security standards?
+
Yes, our experts hold industry-leading certifications including CISSP, CISA, ISO Lead Auditor, and platform-specific certifications from AWS and Microsoft, ensuring your projects are handled by qualified professionals.
