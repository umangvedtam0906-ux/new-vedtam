FAQ

Common Questions About GDPR Mandates

Detailed breakdown of organizational scope, penalty architecture, and implementation speeds.

Who needs to be GDPR compliant?
+
Any business or organization currently processing, profiling, or storing personal data belonging to individuals within the European Union must aggressively abide by GDPR—regardless of where your company is headquartered.
What are the penalties for GDPR non-compliance?
+
Penalties are severe, potentially reaching up to €20 million or 4% of your total global annual turnover (whichever is greater). GDPR also grants individual EU citizens the legal right to sue for compensation directly.
Can you help us if we already have partial compliance?
+
Absolutely. We can drop into your pipeline at any stage—whether constructing from scratch or polishing existing, fragmented efforts into iron-clad, auditable compliance records.
Do you offer support for ongoing GDPR compliance?
+
Yes, continuous support is recommended. Our services include DPO placement, periodic deep audits, documentation updates, and new-staff privacy training cycles to ensure long-term compliance.
What are the core rights of individuals under GDPR?
+
Individuals have the right to access their data, the right to rectification, the right to erasure ('right to be forgotten'), the right to restrict processing, and the right to data portability.
What exactly is a Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA)?
+
A DPIA is a process designed to identify and minimize the data protection risks of a project or system, mandatory for processing likely to result in a high risk to individuals' privacy.
How do you handle international data transfers?
+
We ensure that transfers of personal data outside the EEA are protected by appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or compliance with adequacy decisions.
Is GDPR compliance a one-time event?
+
No, it is an ongoing requirement. As your business processes evolve and new technologies are adopted, your GDPR framework must be continuously reviewed and updated to remain compliant.
